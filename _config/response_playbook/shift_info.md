# Playbook: Shift Information

## Covers These Intents
- "How do shifts work?"
- "What do I do on a shift?"
- "How long are shifts?"
- "What should I wear?"
- "What if I can't make it?"

## General Shift Response
Shifts are typically 2-4 hours. You pick them up in the app based on your location and availability. Each shift shows the date, time, location, pay, and what you'll be doing.

## Circle K Premium
Food prep shifts — you'll be making food items at Circle K stores. Dress code: closed-toe shoes, no loose jewelry. The app will have specific instructions for each shift.

## PepsiCo
Product stocking and merchandising at retail stores. You'll be organizing product displays. Comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes.

## If They Want Specific Shifts
"Want me to show you what's available near you? Just tell me what days work best and I'll pull up options."

## Cancellation Policy
If you can't make a shift, cancel as early as possible in the app. Late cancellations (within 12 hours of start) affect your reliability score, which determines which shifts you see first.
