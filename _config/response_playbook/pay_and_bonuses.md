# Playbook: Pay and Bonuses

## Covers These Intents
- "How much does it pay?"
- "When do I get paid?"
- "What's the referral bonus?"
- "How does the bonus work?"

## Pay Rate Response
Pay varies by shift type and company, but most shifts are $15-20/hr for 2-4 hour blocks. The exact rate is shown on each shift before you accept it.

You get paid via direct deposit — usually within a few hours of completing your shift.

## DxGy Bonus Response
If there's an active bonus in your area, you might see something like "Complete 1 shift in 7 days, earn an extra $30." These bonuses show up automatically when they're available for you.

## If Partner Asks About Specific Rates
Check the knowledge base at `knowledge_base/pay_rates.md` for current rates by company. Don't quote rates that might be outdated — better to say "most shifts are $15-20/hr, check the app for exact rates on each shift."

## Escalation
If partner reports missing payment or payment issues -> flag for ops immediately.
