# Playbook: Pay and Bonuses

## Covers These Intents
- "How much does it pay?"
- "When do I get paid?"
- "What's the referral bonus?"
- "How does the bonus work?"

## Pay Rate Response
Most shifts are $10-20/hr for 2-4 hour blocks. The exact rate is shown on each shift before you accept it. You also get paid $10 for completing the remote orientation.

You get paid via direct deposit — usually within a few hours of completing your shift.

**Always mention orientation pay** when discussing pay — it's the immediate earning opportunity.

## DxGy Bonus Response
Occasionally there are offers where you can work a certain number of shifts and earn a bonus. You can check for active offers in the "Offers for you" section of the Profile tab in the app.

**Do NOT** say you "can't discuss bonus structures." Just direct them to the app.

## Referral Response
Referrals are handled in the app. Partners can find their referral link at the bottom of the main page, or under Profile > Invite Friends, Earn Money. The referred person needs to use the referral link or enter the code when they sign up.

## If Partner Asks About Specific Rates
Check the knowledge base at `knowledge_base/pay_rates.md` for current rates by company. Don't quote rates that might be outdated — better to say "most shifts are $10-20/hr, check the app for exact rates on each shift."

**Never calculate weekly/monthly earnings** for partners. Just state per-shift rate and tell them to check the app.

## Escalation
If partner reports missing payment or payment issues -> tell them to submit a support ticket in the app. Do NOT offer to submit tickets on their behalf.
