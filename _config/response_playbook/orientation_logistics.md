# Playbook: Orientation Logistics

## Covers These Intents
- "When is my orientation?"
- "Where do I go?"
- "How do I sign up for orientation?"
- "I can't find the orientation"
- "What is the orientation?"

## How to Start the In-App Orientation
Open the Shiftsmart app and look for the "In-app orientations" section on the home screen. Tap "Complete learning module" next to Circle K - Food Prep - Breakfast. It takes about 30 minutes. Once you finish and complete your first shift, you'll get a $10 bonus.

## Response Template
The orientation is how you get started on the platform. It's company-specific (e.g. the Circle K orientation for Circle K shifts).

**In-app orientation** — do it from your phone anytime. Takes about 30 minutes. Your progress is saved, so you can start now and finish later. Open the app, look for the "In-app orientations" section, and tap "Complete learning module" next to Circle K - Food Prep - Breakfast. You'll get a $10 bonus after your first shift.

**In-person orientation** — shadow an experienced partner on a real shift. You'll get paid the full shift rate. **Only offer this if confirmed available in the partner's market.** Default to remote.

## If Partner Can't Find Orientation / "Get Started" Button / Says It's Not Working
First response: just restate the steps clearly. Don't troubleshoot yet.
"To get started, open the Shiftsmart app and look for the 'In-app orientations' section on the home screen. Tap 'Complete learning module' next to Circle K - Food Prep - Breakfast."

Only if they come back and say it STILL doesn't work or they can't see the "In-app orientations" section on the home screen, give the alternate path:
"You can also go to the Shifts tab, find a Circle K shift — it'll have a little lock icon. Click on that shift and then click Get Started to go to the Learning Module."

If that still doesn't work, push them toward an in-person orientation:
"No worries — you can also do an in-person orientation where you'd shadow an experienced partner on a real shift. You'd learn everything hands-on and get paid the full shift rate, which is more than the in-app orientation. Want me to find one near you?"

If they say yes → look up the closest stores with upcoming shadow/ambassador orientation shifts and present 2-3 options with distance and date/time.

**NOTE**: Background check is NOT required to start orientation. Do not mention BGC as a prerequisite.

If partner still can't get into any orientation path (remote broken + no in-person available), tell the partner "Let me look into this for you" and flag the conversation for manual review. Do NOT tell them to submit a support ticket.

## Escalation
If partner reports a technical issue that troubleshooting doesn't fix and in-person orientation isn't available -> tell them "Let me look into this for you" and flag the conversation for manual review.
