# Playbook: Orientation Logistics

## Covers These Intents
- "When is my orientation?"
- "Where do I go?"
- "How do I sign up for orientation?"
- "I can't find the orientation"
- "What is the orientation?"

## How to Start the Remote Orientation
Open the Shiftsmart app and on the home screen click "Get Started" on First Shift Requirements, then click "Start Module". It takes about 30 minutes and you get paid $10 for completing it.

## Response Template
The orientation is how you get started on the platform. You have two options:

**Remote orientation** — do it from your phone anytime. Takes about 30 minutes, and you get paid $10 for completing it. Open the app, click "Get Started" on First Shift Requirements, then click "Start Module".

**In-person orientation** — shadow an experienced partner on a real shift. You'll get paid the full shift rate. Check the app for available slots near you.

## If Partner Can't Find Orientation / "Get Started" Button
Try closing and reopening the app. Make sure your profile is complete and your background check is submitted. The "Get Started" button should appear on the home screen under First Shift Requirements. If it's still not showing, let me know and I'll flag it for our team.

## Escalation
If partner reports a technical issue that troubleshooting doesn't fix -> flag for ops with partner_id and description.
