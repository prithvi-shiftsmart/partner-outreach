# Playbook: Orientation Logistics

## Covers These Intents
- "When is my orientation?"
- "Where do I go?"
- "How do I sign up for orientation?"
- "I can't find the orientation"
- "What is the orientation?"

## Response Template
The orientation is how you get started on the platform. You have two options:

**Remote orientation** — do it from your phone anytime. Takes about 30-45 minutes, and you get paid $10 for completing it. Open the app and look for the "Orientation" tab.

**In-person orientation** — shadow an experienced partner on a real shift. You'll get paid the full shift rate. Check the app for available slots near you.

## If Partner Can't Find Orientation Tab
Try closing and reopening the app. If you still don't see it, make sure your profile is complete and your background check is submitted. If it's still not showing, let me know and I'll flag it for our team.

## Escalation
If partner reports a technical issue that troubleshooting doesn't fix -> flag for ops with partner_id and description.
