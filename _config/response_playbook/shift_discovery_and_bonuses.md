# Playbook: Shift Discovery & DxGy Bonuses

## Covers These Intents
- "Send me shifts"
- "How do I find shifts?"
- "Where are the shifts?"
- "There are no shifts"
- "How do I get the bonus?"
- "How do I accept the offer?"

## How to Find Shifts
You can open the Shiftsmart app and on the shifts tab you can see all the different shifts available in your area for each day.

## How to Accept a DxGy Bonus Offer
Go to the Profile tab, under Promos, you will see the "Offers for you" tab and you'll be able to accept the offer there. Then work the required shifts and you'll be eligible for the bonus on top of your shift pay.

## Response: Partner Wants Shifts ("send them" / "how do I find them")
You can open the Shiftsmart app and on the shifts tab you can see all the different shifts available in your area for each day. Your offer is for {company} shifts!

If you go to the Profile tab, under Promos, you will see the "Offers for you" tab and you'll be able to accept the offer there. You can then work three shifts for {company} and you'll be eligible for the ${bonus_amount} bonus on top of your shift pay!

## Response: Partner Says "No Shifts Available"
Don't argue or explain supply/demand. Check if they have any upcoming shifts booked. If they do:
"Looks like you've got one on [day] and more will be uploaded as the week goes by! Thank you for being a regular Shiftsmart partner."

If they truly have nothing:
"More shifts get uploaded throughout the week — check back in the app daily. We'll also let you know when new ones open up near you."

Never promise specific shifts will appear. Never say "shifts are always available."

## Response: Partner Asks About Bonus Details
"Your offer is to complete [X] shifts in the next [Y] days and earn a $[amount] bonus — that's on top of your regular shift pay. Go to Profile → Promos → 'Offers for you' to accept it."

## Key Rules
- Always tell them the specific company their offer is for
- Always direct them to Profile → Promos → "Offers for you" to accept
- When shifts are limited, acknowledge it honestly and point to what IS available
- Thank long-term partners — "Thank you for being a regular Shiftsmart partner"
- Don't oversell availability — if shifts are scarce, say more get uploaded as the week goes on
