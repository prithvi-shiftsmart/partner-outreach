# In-App Orientation Walkthrough — Source of Truth

Partner-facing guide walking through every module in order. The concierge uses this to coach partners who are stuck at a specific step. Modules unlock sequentially — partners must finish the previous module before the next becomes available.

## Overview
- 9 learning modules, ~30 minutes total on the partner's phone
- Entry: Home tab → "Earn $10 today, unlock shifts" section → "Unlock nearby shifts" card → **Start earning**
- After the 9 modules: partner also completes a certification call, profile photo, and background check submission from the same card (~5 min combined)
- Progress saves automatically — partners can pause and resume anytime
- $10 paid after the partner completes their first shift (not upon orientation completion)

# Food Prep Onboarding — Module Completion Guide

This guide walks through every module in order, with the exact steps required to mark each one complete. Modules unlock sequentially — you must finish the previous module before the next becomes available.

---

## 1. Shiftsmart Introduction
**Time:** ~3 min · **Sections:** Introduction only

**How to complete:**
1. Open the module from the home list.
2. Read or skim each intro slide.
3. Tap **Continue / Next** through every slide until the final one.
4. Tap **Finish** on the last slide — the module flips to Completed and returns you home.

---

## 2. Using the Unox Oven
**Time:** ~5 min · **Sections:** Watch & Learn → Guided Tutorial → Solo Practice (Easy → Medium → Hard)

### Watch & Learn
1. Tap **Watch & Learn**.
2. Play each video in the list to the end (the next video unlocks as the previous finishes).
3. When the last video ends you'll be taken to the Module Detail screen automatically.

### Guided Tutorial
Follow McKenna's prompts for all 16 oven tasks. Highlights:
1. Tap **Circle K** on the home screen, then **Sandwiches** to enter temp selection.
2. Tap to speed up preheating.
3. Tap **Row 1** → tap the **Blue/Pink** sandwich preset to start the timer.
4. Tap **Row 3** → tap the **Burrito THW** preset.
5. Speed up the cook timer, then take the items out.
6. Hold **Start/Stop** to return to home.
7. Tap **Circle K** → **Circle K (frozen items)** category.
8. Set **Chicken Bites** in Row 2 and **Hashbrown** in Row 4.
9. Replace the Hashbrown timer with **Pretzel** on Row 4.
10. Speed up timer, take items out, hold **Start/Stop** to finish.

### Solo Practice
- **Easy:** Cook 2 trays of Blue/Pink sandwiches.
- **Medium:** Set temps for Chicken Bites, Apple Pie, Frozen Corn Dog, and Homestyle Chicken Sandwich; then cook 2 Red/Gold sandwiches.
- **Hard:** Bake any cookie tray; cook a Godfather Pizza + Hashbrowns + Chicken Wings + Egg Rolls; then cook one each of Blue/Pink, Red/Gold, and Burrito sandwiches.

Finish all three difficulties to mark the module complete.

## 3. Print Labels from Upshop
**Time:** ~5 min · **Sections:** Watch & Learn → Guided Tutorial → Solo Practice (Easy → Medium → Hard)

### Guided Tutorial (9 steps)
1. Tap the **Tasks** nav button.
2. Open the **6 AM Cook List**.
3. Tap **Start Cook Plan**.
4. Print your first label: **Beef Empanada**.
5. Open **Chargrilled Burger**, change quantity to **4**, print.
6. Open **Premium Hot Dog**, change quantity to **10**, print.
7. Tap the home/back button to return to the Home Screen.
8. Tap **Print Labels** in the nav.
9. Find **Bacon Sausage Cheddar Pretzel Sandwich** and print.

### Solo Practice
- **Easy:** From the 6 AM Cook List, print **4 Cheesy Pepper Jack Tornado** labels.
- **Medium:** From the 10 AM Cook List, print **6 Cheesy Pepper Jack Tornado**, **4 Hashbrown**, and **6 Chicken Wings** labels (the suggested defaults will be wrong — change them).
- **Hard:** Print **2 Brunch Burger**, **4 Ranchero Tornado**, **5 Chicken Bites**, then open **Print Labels** and print **3 Bacon Sausage Cheddar Pretzel Sandwich** labels.

## 4. Print Labels from Menu Pilot
**Time:** ~3 min · **Sections:** Watch & Learn → Guided Tutorial → Solo Practice (Easy only)

### Guided Tutorial (8 steps)
1. Tap the **Hot Food** category.
2. Open the **Chicken** subcategory.
3. Tap **CK Crispy Bites**.
4. Tap **Print**.
5. Tap **Back** to return home.
6. Use the search bar to find **Brunch Burger**.
7. Change quantity to **2**.
8. Tap **Print**.

### Solo Practice (Easy)
Print labels for the listed items at the correct quantities — CK Crispy Chicken Bites (1), CK Homestyle Chicken Sandwich w/ Pickles (2), Godfather's Pepperoni Mini Pizza (2).

## 5. Scan Items
**Time:** ~5 min · **Sections:** Watch & Learn → Guided Tutorial → Solo Practice (Medium only)

### Guided Tutorial
1. Scan each item that was cooked using the on-screen scanner.
2. Continue scanning until all cooked items are logged.
3. Open **Review Scanned Items**.
4. For any items not scanned, log the exception (e.g., burned, dropped, ran out).

### Solo Practice (Medium)
Scan all cooked items AND log exceptions for everything that wasn't cooked. Module marks complete when the scan list is fully resolved.

## 7. Package & Label Items
**Time:** ~5 min · **Sections:** Video Introduction → Package Review → Package Matching Game → Guided Tutorial

### Video Introduction & Package Review

### Package Matching Game (timed)

## 8. Shift Walkthrough
**Time:** ~5 min · **Sections:** Guided Tutorial only (39 tasks across a full simulated shift)

### Block 1 — Selfie & Start Shift
1. Tap **Lets get started** on McKenna's intro popup (or tap outside to dismiss; auto-dismisses after 8s).
2. Tap **Start** on **Take a Selfie**, then **Get started**.
3. Review the dress code → **UP NEXT**.
4. Tap the **Add photo** area, then **UP NEXT** to finish the selfie.
5. Tap **Start** on **Start your shift**, then **Get started**.

### Block 2 — Setup
6. Read **Greet Manager** → **UP NEXT**.
7. Read **Confirm Unox oven is on** → **UP NEXT**.
8. Tap the **Preheat the oven** button (just tap — no real preheating).
9. Read **Store Walkthrough** intro → **UP NEXT**.
10. Review the location list → **UP NEXT**.
11. Read **Be Food Safe!** → **UP NEXT**.

### Block 3 — Cook Sandwiches (Task List → Cook Sandwiches)
12. Tap **Start** on **Cook Sandwiches**, then **Get started**.
13. Read **Find Batch A bins** → **UP NEXT**.
14. Read **Find Sandwiches in Cold Case** (tap the photo to enlarge if needed) → **UP NEXT**.
15. Read **Put sandwiches on trays** → **UP NEXT**.
16. Read **Put Tray 1 in oven** → **UP NEXT**.
17. Tap the button to **Set the timer** for Tray 1.
18. Read **Cook Remaining Sandwiches** → **UP NEXT**.
19. Tap **UP NEXT** on the congrats screen.

### Block 4 — Hot Dogs
20. Tap **Get started** for **Set Hot dogs out**.
21. Read **Find Hot Dog bins** → **UP NEXT**.
22. Tap the button to **Place hot dogs on roller grill**.
23. Tap **UP NEXT** on **Take Photo of Roller Grill**.
24. Tap **UP NEXT** on the congrats screen.

### Block 5 — Print Labels
25. Tap **Get started** for **Setup and print labels**.
26. Read **Find and log into store iPad** → **UP NEXT**.
27. Tap the button to **Print labels**.
28. Tap **UP NEXT** on the congrats screen.

### Block 6 — Label & Stock
29. Tap **Get started** for **Label and stock items**.
30. Read **Label Sandwiches** → **UP NEXT**.
31. Tap the **photo area**, then **UP NEXT**.
32. Tap the button to **Scan and log items**.
33. Read **Stock Hot Case** → **UP NEXT**.

### Block 7 — Finish
34. Tap **Get Started** on **Finish and check out**.
35. Read the **Final notes** → **UP NEXT**.
36. Tap the button on **Check Out**.
37. Slide the **Check Out** slider all the way across.
38. On the checkout popup, tap **Return to Homescreen**.

> The module **only** marks complete when you finish the Check-Out flow above. Tapping the back arrow on the shift home screen exits without completing.

## 9. Learning Review (Final Quiz)
**Time:** ~2 min · **Sections:** Quick Learning Review

**How to complete:**
1. Answer each multiple-choice question.
2. If you get one wrong you'll see the correct answer; tap **Next** to continue.
3. After the last question, your score is shown and the module marks complete.

## Tips for All Modules
- **Sequential locking** — Locked modules show as compact rows with title, time, and a "Locked" badge.
- **In-progress** — A module showing a progress bar means you've already started it; pick up where you left off.
- **Popups** — Most McKenna popups can be dismissed by tapping the screen behind them, and most auto-dismiss within 4–8 seconds.
- **Photos** — Any photo with a slight border can usually be tapped to view full-screen.
- **Returning home** — At any time tap the back arrow in the top-left to exit a module without losing progress.

## Coaching Tips by Situation (concierge-facing)

### Partner stalled on a specific module
Check which module they're on and how long they've been there. Pull the module-specific steps above and give them the next action:
- "You're on module [X] — takes about [Y] minutes. [specific next step]. Want to keep going?"

### Partner says "it's too long"
"You can pause and come back anytime — your progress is saved. You're on module [X] of 9. Finish up and you can start picking up shifts — plus a $10 bonus after your first one."

### Partner says "I don't understand this module"
Give them the module-specific steps from above. If still stuck: "No worries — when you get to your first shift, this will all make sense. The app walks you through everything in real time too."

### Partner says "the module won't load / is broken"
"Try closing and reopening the app. If it still won't load, you can submit a support ticket in the app."

### Partner finished all 9 modules
"Nice work. Head back to the 'Unlock nearby shifts' card on the Home tab to wrap up the certification call, profile photo, and background check — those take about 5 minutes combined. Once you submit the background check, you can start picking up shifts right away while it's being reviewed."
