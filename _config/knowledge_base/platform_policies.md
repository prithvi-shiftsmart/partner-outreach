# Platform Policies — Source of Truth

Sourced from Support team knowledge base. Use this to answer partner questions about how the platform works, account rules, and what to expect.

## How Shiftsmart Works
- 4 steps to join: download app + profile → project onboarding → shift selection → payment
- Partners are **independent contractors** — not employees. Never use "employee", "salary", "training", or "hired"
- No minimum or maximum work requirement — work as much or as little as you want
- Payment in days, not weeks

## Signup Requirements
- Phone number, first/last name, email, birthdate, password
- Must enable location services and notifications
- 6-digit verification code sent to phone
- Must agree to Partner Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
- VOIP / TextNow numbers are NOT allowed — must be a real cell phone number

## One Account Policy
- Strictly limited to ONE account per partner
- Sharing credentials or creating multiple accounts = fraud = immediate deactivation
- Bank account can only be associated with one Stripe account

## Background Check (BGC)
- Required before working shifts
- Processed through Checkr — usually completes within 7 business days
- If pending > 14 days: contact Checkr directly at candidate.checkr.com
- Cannot work shifts until BGC status is "Clear"
- 18-hour rule: system auto-removes from shifts 18hrs before start if BGC not cleared
- Under 18: requires parental consent form

## Attendance & Reliability
- Check in within 10 minutes of shift start time
- Late arrival: > 10 minutes past start
- Late cancellation: < 12 hours before start
- Both impact Reliability Score automatically
- Must confirm shifts 12+ hours prior — removed if not confirmed and shift is reassigned
- Standard cancellation notice: 24 hours

## Rewards Program
- **Gold**: 200 points in 14 days → top-tier rewards, faster/cheaper instant pay, raffles
- **Silver**: 100 points in 14 days → perks, faster instant pay
- Earn points by: picking up shifts without bonus, showing up on time, completing shifts

## Referrals
- No limit on total referrals
- Referred person must use referral link or enter code during signup
- Referral link can be found at the bottom of the main page, or under Profile > Invite Friends, Earn Money
- Must complete first verified non-training shift within 30 days
- Payment: 24-48 hours after verified shift completion
- Bonus amounts vary by market
- Prohibited: paid ads, job boards (Indeed, Craigslist, ZipRecruiter), self-referrals
- Referral codes must be entered during signup — no retroactive application

## Account Management
- Disable account: Profile → Settings → Disable Account
- Request deletion: Profile → Settings → Request Account Deletion → choose "Delete my data" or "Get a copy of my data"
- Can rejoin anytime by creating a new account

## Dress Code
- Polos or plain shirts, pants without holes, closed-toe shoes
- Bring a sweater for cooler environments

## Phone Requirements
- Fully functional smartphone with reliable data plan required
- Battery must be 80%+ at shift start
- Must have cellular data (not Wi-Fi only) — GPS tracking required
- Location permissions must be set to "Precise Location"
- "Always On" location recommended

## Turn-Aways
- If store is closed or refuses service: report via in-app chat at beginning of shift
- Must provide photo proof (store hours sign or locked doors)
- Wait at least 20 minutes before leaving
- Turn-away pay issued after verification
- No reimbursement for gas or travel

## Suspensions vs Deactivation
- Temporary suspension: typically 7 days, auto-reactivates
- Deactivation: I can't help with that from here — direct partner to submit a support ticket in the app
- Causes: late cancellations, no-shows, fraud, policy violations
- **Never tell a partner deactivation is "permanent"** — just say you can't help from here and direct to app support

## Cross-Company Work
- Partners can work shifts for multiple companies (Circle K, Dollar General, PepsiCo, etc.)
- Each company requires its own orientation — a Circle K orientation does NOT qualify you for Dollar General shifts
- If partner is employed by a company (e.g. Dollar General manager), they cannot do Shiftsmart shifts for that same company, but CAN do shifts for other companies
