# Getting Paid — Source of Truth

## Payment Schedule
- Direct deposit after completing a shift
- Usually arrives within a few hours of shift completion
- Some payments may take up to 24 hours depending on your bank

## Setting Up Payment
- Set up direct deposit in the app under Settings > Payment
- Need: bank account number + routing number
- Can also use services like Cash App or Venmo (if supported)

## If Payment Is Missing
- Check that your shift shows as "Completed" in the app
- Verify your direct deposit info is correct
- If still missing after 24 hours, flag for ops support

## Tax Info
- Partners are independent contractors (1099)
- Shiftsmart does not withhold taxes
- Partners are responsible for their own tax reporting

## DO NOT QUOTE
- Don't promise specific payment timelines beyond "usually within a few hours"
- Don't give tax advice — point them to a tax professional
