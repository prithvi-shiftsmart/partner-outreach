# In-App Orientation Walkthrough — Source of Truth

Use this to coach partners through the in-app orientation module by module. If a partner is stuck, you can tell them exactly what to expect in their current module and what comes next.

## Overview
- 9 modules, ~30 minutes total
- Done on their phone in the Shiftsmart app
- Partner gets paid $10 after completing their first shift (not upon orientation completion)
- Can pause and resume anytime — picks up where they left off
- Each module has: intro video → guided practice → timed challenge (for skill modules)

## Module-by-Module Guide

### Module 1: Shiftsmart Introduction (~5 min)
**What it is**: Introductory video about Shiftsmart and Circle K food prep shifts.
**What to tell partners**: "This is just a quick intro video — watch it and you're done with module 1."
**Common issue**: None — this is the easy one.

### Module 2: Using the Unox Oven (~10 min)
**What it is**: Video overview + interactive practice using the oven controls.
**Steps**: Reset oven (Start/Stop button) → tap Circle K → select Sandwiches → set temperature → set timer per row.
**Timed challenge**: Set temperature and timer under time pressure (easy → medium → hard).
**What to tell partners**: "You'll practice setting up the oven on your phone — it walks you through the buttons step by step. The timed challenge just means you do it a bit faster each round."
**Common issue**: Partners get confused by the two settings (Sandwiches vs Circle K). Remind them: sandwiches go under Sandwiches setting, everything else (including chicken sandwich) goes under Circle K.

### Module 3: Print Labels from Upshop (~8 min)
**What it is**: Video + practice navigating the Upshop iPad app to print labels.
**Steps**: Open Upshop → find cook list → select items + quantities → print labels.
**What to tell partners**: "This teaches you how to use the store iPad to print labels for the food you cook. It's like a step-by-step simulator."
**Common issue**: Partners don't realize this is practice — they sometimes think they need an actual iPad.

### Module 4: Assemble Items (~10 min)
**What it is**: Videos + practice for assembling chicken sandwiches, pretzels, and pizzas.
**Note**: This module may be under construction — if a partner reports it's incomplete, tell them to skip what they can and move on.
**What to tell partners**: "This shows you how to put together some of the menu items — chicken sandwiches, pretzels, that kind of thing."

### Module 5: Scan Items (~5 min)
**What it is**: Video + practice scanning barcodes, handling quantity mismatches, selecting reason codes.
**What to tell partners**: "After you cook items, you scan each barcode in the app to log what you made. This module teaches you how. It's quick."
**Common issue**: The concept of "reason codes" (why scanned quantity differs from expected) can be confusing. Tell them: "If you couldn't cook something because it was out of stock, you just pick 'out of stock' as the reason."

### Module 6: Determine Correct Packaging (~6 min)
**What it is**: Photos of food items matched to correct packaging. Interactive matching exercises.
**What to tell partners**: "This shows you which box or cup each item goes in. Chicken bites go in a cup, wings go in a box, that kind of thing."
**Common issue**: Partners rush through this and get the timed challenge wrong. Tell them: "Take a quick look at the photos first — the packaging types are pretty straightforward."

### Module 7: Put Labels on Packaging (~5 min)
**What it is**: Videos + practice for correct label placement on each package type.
**Key rule**: Always cover the old barcode with the new printed label.
**What to tell partners**: "This teaches you where to stick the label on each package. The main thing to remember: cover the old barcode."

### Module 8: Shift Walkthrough (~10 min)
**What it is**: A guided tap-through of a full practice shift, start to finish.
**Steps**: Check in → selfie → greet manager → preheat oven → store walkthrough → cook sandwiches → hot dogs → print labels → label + stock items → check out.
**What to tell partners**: "This is a practice run of your whole shift — just tap through each step. It shows you exactly what to expect on your first day."
**Common issue**: This is the longest module. Partners sometimes think it's a real shift. Remind them: "This is just practice on your phone — you're not at a store yet."

### Module 9: Learning Review (~2 min)
**What it is**: Short quiz to confirm knowledge before first shift.
**What to tell partners**: "Quick quiz at the end — just a few questions based on what you just learned. If you paid attention to the modules, you'll be fine."

## Coaching Tips by Situation

### Partner stalled on a specific module
Check which module they're on and how long they've been there. Give them the module-specific guidance above plus encouragement:
- "You're on module [X] — that one takes about [Y] minutes. [specific guidance]. Want to keep going?"

### Partner says "it's too long"
"You can pause and come back anytime — your progress is saved. You're on module [X] of 9 — [remaining] modules left. Finish up and you can start picking up shifts — plus a $10 bonus after your first one."

### Partner says "I don't understand this module"
Give them the module-specific explanation from above. If they're still stuck: "No worries — when you get to your first shift, this will all make sense. The app walks you through everything in real time too."

### Partner says "the module won't load / is broken"
"Try closing and reopening the app. If it still won't load, you can submit a support ticket in the app."

### Partner finished all 9 modules
"You're done with orientation! You're now eligible to pick up shifts — and you'll get a $10 bonus after your first one. Want me to show you what's available near you?"
