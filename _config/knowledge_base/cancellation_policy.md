# Cancellation Policy — Source of Truth

## Cancelling a Shift

- There are no penalty fees for cancellations — cancellations affect your reliability score only
- Cancel at least 24 hours before your shift to minimize impact on your reliability score
- Same-day cancellations hurt your reliability score and can lead to suspension
- **No-shows** have the most severe impact on your reliability score and can lead to suspension

## How to Cancel via Concierge

- Text "I need to cancel" — the concierge will handle the cancellation
- After cancellation, the concierge will offer **3 alternative shifts** to rebook

## How to Cancel via App

- Go to the **Schedule tab** → tap the shift → tap **Cancel**

## Rebooking Limit

- One rebooking attempt via the concierge — if the partner cancels again after rebooking, they are directed to manage future changes in the app directly

## Reliability Impact

- Cancellations affect your reliability score — cancellations do NOT incur a fee
- Cancel at least 24 hours before your shift to minimize impact
- Same-day cancellations and no-shows hurt your score and can lead to suspension
- No-shows have the most severe impact
- Higher Reliability and On Time scores unlock early access to shifts, new project opportunities, and free or discounted Instant Pay

## Shift Timing Rules

- You can clock out whenever you want — you're only paid for the time you're checked in
- If you're more than 20 minutes late to the start of your shift, you may be removed from the shift

## DO NOT QUOTE

- Don't mention penalty fees — there are none. Cancellations affect reliability score only.
- Don't frame cancellation as "no big deal" — it does affect reliability and future shift access
