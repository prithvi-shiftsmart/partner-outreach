# Payment Details — Source of Truth

Sourced from Support team knowledge base. Use for detailed payment questions beyond the basics in pay_rates.md.

## Payment Method
- Payments via Stripe to partner's own bank account or debit card
- Must use your own unique bank account — cannot use someone else's
- Bank account can only be associated with one Stripe account and one Partner ID

## Instant Pay
- Available after shift completion
- $1.99 flat fee per transaction
- Daily limit: one withdrawal per calendar day (resets midnight PST)
- Card aging: newly added debit cards ineligible for 7 days
- Once Standard payout is initiated, cannot be converted to Instant
- If "Sent to Transit": funds left our system, being processed by bank

## Standard Pay
- 2-3 business days for bank transfers
- No fee

## Payment Eligibility
- Only paid for fully completed tasks that meet criteria
- No payment for incomplete work
- Shifts without completed task list require manual verification (up to 48 hours)
- Photos must be submitted in-app during shift — emailed photos rejected unless tech error reported within 30 mins

## Payment Issues
- If payment sent to wrong card/account: cannot reverse once initiated. Contact bank.
- If transaction fails: funds reset, can withdraw to different card
- Receipt disputes: must submit in writing within 3 business days
- Missing payment investigation: wait 48 hours before escalating

## Fraud Flags
- Shifts flagged when: tasks not completed on-site, photos faked/reused, not on-site during shift
- Flagged ≠ fraud confirmed — just means extra review (24-72 hours)
- If confirmed fraud: shift rejected, no payment, possible suspension or ban
- Avoid flags: stay on site full shift, complete 100% of task list

## Tax Info
- Partners are 1099 independent contractors
- Shiftsmart does NOT withhold taxes
- 1099 form created if earned $600+ in tax year
- Physical copies mailed by 1/31 federal deadline
- Digital copies available via Stripe email link
- Work history available in app dashboard
- DO NOT calculate taxes for partners — tell them to consult a tax professional

## 40-Hour Cap
- Weekly cap across the platform to ensure fairness
- Back-to-back / double shifts permitted within brand guidelines
- If partner hits cap, explain the policy — don't promise exceptions
