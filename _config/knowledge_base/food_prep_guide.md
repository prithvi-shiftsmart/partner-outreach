# Food Prep Shift Guide — Source of Truth

This is the full reference for Circle K Premium food prep shifts. Use this to answer partner questions about what to expect on shift, how equipment works, and how to handle common situations.

## Shift Flow (In Order)

1. Arrive, greet the manager, ask about the food program today
2. Turn on Unox oven, start preheating (Sandwiches setting)
3. Store walkthrough — find bins, trays, packaging, iPad, label printers
4. Turn on warmers: hot case, roller grill, pizza case
5. Wash hands, hairnet, gloves
6. Gather and cook Batch A sandwiches (load oven, set timers)
7. While sandwiches cook: prep hot dogs on roller grill + print labels
8. When sandwiches done: label, scan, stock in hot case
9. Move hot dogs from back to front of roller grill
10. Reset oven to Circle K (frozen), preheat for Batch C
11. Cook Batch C frozen snacks (chicken bites, wings, etc.)
12. Package, label, scan, stock frozen items
13. Repeat for remaining batches, then check out

## Equipment

### Unox Oven
- Two temperature settings: **Sandwiches** and **Circle K**
- Sandwiches (except chicken sandwich) cook under **Sandwiches** setting
- Chicken sandwich and all other items cook under **Circle K** setting
- To turn on: press black power button on bottom left of oven door
- To reset: hold Start/Stop button for 5 seconds until you hear a beep
- To wake screen: hold home button for 15 seconds
- To set timer: tap empty row, select item preset, timer starts automatically

### Hot Case / Roller Grill / Pizza Case
- Turn on via switches under the metal drawer
- Pizza case: switch on top back of case
- Pizza case needs water: pour into funnel on top back until water light turns off
- If broken: speak with Store Manager, confirm work order, mark as equipment issue

### iPad / Upshop App
- Passcode: usually last 4 digits of store number (on back of iPad)
- To print labels: Tasks → select cook list (5am/10am/2pm) → Start Plan → printer icon → enter quantity
- On-demand printing: Print Labels → search for item
- If store uses Menu Pilot instead of Upshop: similar interface, find item and press print

### Label Printer
- If not working: hold button on bottom right until it turns blue/green to reset
- If out of labels: find label paper in prep area or back room, open from front to restock

## Cooking Rules

- **Always use metal trays** — never plastic
- Basket trays for sandwiches, flat trays for everything (can also do sandwiches)
- Do NOT unwrap sandwiches before cooking
- Hot dogs go directly on roller grill (back first), NOT in the oven
- Up to 9 sandwiches per tray, keep same flavors together
- Always cover old barcode with new printed label
- Scan everything you cook — this is how you get full credit and faster payment

## Chicken Sandwich Assembly
Cook chicken patty in oven (Circle K setting, NOT Sandwiches). Find buns, pickles, butter spray. Each sandwich: butter on inside of buns, two pickles, close and bag.

## Pretzel Assembly
Cook in oven. When out: spray with butter spray, apply seasoning, put in bag.

## Common Situations

- **Store already cooked some food**: Cook what the Shiftsmart app tells you. If store staff asks you not to cook something, log it as "store request"
- **Store not letting me cook**: Be respectful. Log as store request. Submit ticket after shift.
- **Too many items to cook**: Use at least 3 racks, multitask while oven runs. Can work up to 15 min past scheduled end (paid for those 15 min, not more).
- **Store staff asks for non-cooking tasks**: As independent contractor, you don't have to. If you agree, you still get paid. Submit ticket saying you were turned away from cooking.
- **Can't scan barcodes**: Tap "Update item amount" to manually enter cooked items.
- **Dropped food**: Never put it out for sale. Alert store staff to write it off.
- **Hurt on shift**: Tell store manager, seek care. Submit ticket in app.
- **Store staff is rude**: Stay respectful, complete as much as possible. Submit ticket after.
- **Follow Upshop or Shiftsmart app?**: Always follow the Shiftsmart app.

## Food Safety
- Wash hands before handling food
- Hairnet required (hat as backup if no hairnets available)
- Gloves required
- Temp checks: JRI app → Checklist → select location → select item type → probe item → Done
- Clean dishes in 3-compartment sink: wash with green soap → rinse → sanitize 30 seconds → air dry
- If no sanitizer water in third sink: fill half to 2/3 with cold water, add two capfuls of sanitizer
- Clean thermometer with alcohol wipes (or soap and water if no wipes)
