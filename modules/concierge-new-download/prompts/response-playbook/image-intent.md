# Playbook: Image / Screenshot Intent

## Covers These Intents
- "sent you a pic" / "I sent you a picture"
- "see the screenshot" / "look at this" / "can you see this"
- "did you get the image"
- Any indication that the partner has attached an image

## Behavioral Rule
Fire IMMEDIATELY on the first mention. Do NOT loop on generic guidance first. The concierge cannot view images, so the only useful next step is to ask the partner to describe what they see.

## Canonical Reply
> I can't see images, but if you describe what's on the screen, I'll point you there.

## Do Not
- Continue troubleshooting based on the assumption you saw the image
- Loop on the same generic guidance you gave before the partner sent the pic
- Suggest the partner re-send the image (they already did)
- Tell the partner to email or upload the image elsewhere
