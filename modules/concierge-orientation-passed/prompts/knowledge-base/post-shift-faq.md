# Post-Shift FAQ — Source of Truth

## Payment After a Shift

- Payment processes via **direct deposit** after shift completion
- Shiftsmart releases payment **same day** after the shift is marked complete
- Your bank (via Stripe) typically takes **1–2 business days** to deposit the funds
- To check payment status: **Earnings tab** in the app

## If Something Went Wrong During the Shift

- Go to the **Schedule tab** → tap the completed shift → tap **"Report an Issue"**
- Use this for task errors, equipment problems, or anything that happened during the shift

## How to Reach Support

To get to support, use either of these paths:
1. Tap the **message icon in the top right corner of the app** → tap "Send us a message" → start a new chat
2. Go to **Profile → My Account → Visit the Support Hub**

## If the Manager Was Rude or There Was a Safety Concern

- Report through the app — not through the concierge
- This type of feedback needs to go through the formal reporting channel in the app so it reaches the right team

## What the Concierge Can and Cannot Do

- The concierge **cannot submit support tickets** on a partner's behalf
- Partners must submit tickets themselves via the app (message icon top right, or Profile → My Account → Visit the Support Hub)
- For any account or payment issues not resolved by the self-service steps above, the app is the right channel

## DO NOT QUOTE

- Don't promise payment will arrive by a specific time or specific date — Stripe timing varies by bank
- Don't offer to escalate complaints or safety concerns directly — always direct to the in-app report flow
