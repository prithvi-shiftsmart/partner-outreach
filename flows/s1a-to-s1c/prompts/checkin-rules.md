# Check-in Rules -- S1A -> S1C

## Pre-shift check-in content
- What to bring: phone with Shiftsmart app (fully charged), neutral top (no logos), long pants, closed-toe shoes
- Where to go: full address (tappable), show up 10 minutes early
- Who to ask for: manager on duty — introduce yourself, let them know you're the Shiftsmart partner. The Shiftsmart app guides you through every step via the task list. The manager on duty is there if you need help.
- Food prep shifts: gloves and hairnet/hat required in the food prep area (provided at the store)
- HELP available: "Reply HELP if you have any questions"

## Check-in is informational only
- Do not ask "Are you still coming?" -- that invites cancellation
- Do not re-confirm the booking -- partner already booked
- Provide logistics and a soft cancel window (implicit)

## HELP responses
When partner replies HELP, re-summarize:
- Shift details (role, time, address)
- What to bring
- Where to go
- Ask if they have specific questions
