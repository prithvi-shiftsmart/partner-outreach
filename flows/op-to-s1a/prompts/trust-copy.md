# Trust Copy -- Booking Confirmation

## After successful booking
Include in the confirmation message:
- Full shift details (role, date, time, address)
- Trust line: "Heads up: we add an extra 30 minutes to your first shift so you can settle in and learn the ropes. No pressure to be perfect day one."
- Reminder commitment: "I'll send a reminder 24 hours before with everything you need to know."

## Purpose
- Reduce anxiety about the first shift
- Set expectation that there will be a pre-shift check-in
- Reinforce that the partner has support

## Tone
- Warm but not over-the-top
- Factual -- the extra 30 minutes is real, not a platitude
- No emojis, no exclamation points unless echoing partner energy
