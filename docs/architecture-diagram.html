5 Key Architectural Decisions

Architecture
Subfolder split with selective injection — one common/ base, deal-specific KB loaded per zone
Preference
Partner preference first, fill-rate used only as tiebreaker
Both-Deal Zones
Always ask, never guess — present explicit choice when partner requests help
Cross-Sell
Maximum once per conversation, only at a natural moment after primary orientation
Language
Partner-friendly framing — no jargon like “product rotation”
Orientation
Profile photo removed from flow — 3 steps, not 4
